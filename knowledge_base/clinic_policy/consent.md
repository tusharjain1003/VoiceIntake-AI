# Patient Consent Policy for Voice Intake

## Consent Requirements
1. Patients must be informed that the intake conversation is recorded and transcribed.
2. Data collected during intake is stored securely and shared only with the treating clinician and authorized clinical staff.
3. No health data is used for training or research without explicit written consent.

## Verbal Consent Script
The following must be played at the start of each intake session: "This conversation will be recorded and transcribed for your medical record. Your information is private and will only be shared with your care team. Do you consent to proceed?"

## Opt-Out
If the patient declines consent, the session is terminated, no data is saved, and the patient is directed to call the clinic directly at (555) 000-0001 to schedule by phone.

## Data Retention
Intake recordings and transcripts are retained for the duration required by state law (typically 7 years after the date of service) and then securely deleted.
