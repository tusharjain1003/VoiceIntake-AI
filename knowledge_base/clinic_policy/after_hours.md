# After-Hours Policy

## Scope
This policy applies to all intake conversations initiated outside of normal business hours (08:00–18:00, Monday–Friday).

## Protocol
1. Intake conversations can proceed as normal during after-hours. The voice assistant continues collecting all required fields.
2. If the patient indicates an urgent or emergency concern, the system must flag the conversation for priority review.
3. Scheduled appointments are created in the next available business-day slot unless the patient requests a specific time.
4. CRITICAL red-flag findings (per escalation protocol) bypass the queue and are sent immediately to the on-call clinician via pager or SMS.

## Escalation Contact
The on-call clinician is reached through the answering service: (555) 000-0000. The escalation message includes a summary of the intake data and the specific red-flag trigger.
